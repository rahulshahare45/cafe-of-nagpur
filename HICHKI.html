About Us!....

Hitchki is a Bollywood-themed resto-bar in Nagpur that combines traditional Indian flavors with contemporary culinary techniques, offering a unique fusion dining experience. The establishment features a vibrant ambiance adorned with Bollywood memorabilia, creating a nostalgic atmosphere for patrons. The menu showcases a variety of regional and eclectic dishes, each with a modern twist, alongside an array of innovative cocktails and mocktails. Hitchki also hosts themed nights, such as Bollywood and retro nights, enhancing the dining experience with music and entertainment.
