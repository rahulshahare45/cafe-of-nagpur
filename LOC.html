About Us!....

Life of Chai (LOC) is a popular café chain in Nagpur, established in January 2019 with its first outlet at Manewada Square. Over the years, it has expanded to over 25 branches across Nagpur and Vidarbha, serving thousands of customers daily. LOC offers a youthful ambiance, authentic flavors, and a pocket-friendly menu, making it a favored hangout spot for tea enthusiasts. The café emphasizes community building, providing a space where people can connect and enjoy a variety of teas and snacks. Additionally, LOC supports local vendors and encourages young entrepreneurs, reflecting its commitment to the local community
