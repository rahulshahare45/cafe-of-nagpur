About Us!....

Farzi Café in Nagpur is a modern Indian bistro that blends traditional Indian flavors with contemporary cooking techniques, offering a unique dining experience. Located at 236, 9th Floor, West High Building, WHC Road, Dharampeth, Nagpur, the café features a sleek and contemporary décor with an open kitchen, allowing diners to watch their food being prepared. The menu includes a mix of small plates, entrées, and desserts designed for sharing, along with a variety of traditional Indian cocktails and mocktails. The casual and lively atmosphere makes it a great spot for a night out with friends or special occasions
