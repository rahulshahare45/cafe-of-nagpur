About Us!....

Dabo Club and Kitchen, located at 6, New Manish Nagar, Somalwada, Nagpur, Maharashtra 440025, is a premier nightlife venue that seamlessly blends dining with entertainment. The establishment offers a diverse menu, including North Indian, Continental, and Chinese cuisines, catering to a wide range of tastes. As Nagpur's first night club, it features a vibrant ambiance with both indoor and outdoor seating options, making it an ideal spot for social gatherings and special occasions. The venue is open from 4 PM to 1:30 AM, Tuesday through Sunday.
